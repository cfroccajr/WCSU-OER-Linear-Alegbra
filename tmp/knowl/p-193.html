If \(T\) is a linear transformation from \(\mathbb{R}^m\) to \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) then it may be represented as multiplication by

\begin{equation*} A= \left( \begin{array}{cccc} T(\vec{e}_1) \amp T(\vec{e}_2) \amp \cdots \amp T(\vec{e}_m)\\ \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}

in which each column is the image of one of the elementary basis vectors for \(\mathbb{R}^m\text{.}\)
