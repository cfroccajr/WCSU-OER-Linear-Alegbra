If \(A\) is a diagonalizable matrix so that

\begin{equation*} A=PDP^{-1} \end{equation*}

for some diagonal matrix \(D\text{,}\) then:

  • the entries in \(D\) are the eigenvalues of \(A\)
  • the columns of \(P\) are eigenvectors of \(A\)
  • \(A^{-1}=PD^{-1}P^{-1}\text{,}\) if \(A\) is invertible
  • \(A^n=PD^nP^{-1}\)
